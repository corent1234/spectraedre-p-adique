Dear members of the program committee of ISSAC 2026

We would like to nominate Corentin Cornou (currently master student at ENS Paris-Saclay) for his key contributions to our joint paper “On semidefinite-representable sets over valued fields”.

This work deals with geometrical and algorithmic aspects of linear and semidefinite programming. In particular, in the paper we define polyhedra and spectrahedra over fields with valuation and develop an algorithm for linear programming in this setting. We also on the extension of properties of classical polyhedra and spectrahedra to the valued case.

Corentin Cornou's contributions have been decisive almost everywhere in this paper. This project started in the summer 2023 when Corentin Cornou was appointed as L3 internship student at XLIM, University of Limoges, advised by us, Tristan Vaccon and Simone Naldi.
